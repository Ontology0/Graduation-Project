# CLAUDE.md

## Snapshot Release Conventions

- Keep repository layout centered on `ontology/`, `rag/`, `finetuning/`, `eval/`, `data/`, and `results/`.
- Do not modify `README.md` content during snapshot maintenance tasks.
- Put ontology resources and schema-related research artifacts in `ontology/`.
- Put retrieval pipelines and retrieval experiments in `rag/`.
- Put model adaptation scripts and configs in `finetuning/`.
- Put benchmark and judge/evaluation scripts in `eval/`.
- Put datasets and preprocess outputs in `data/`.
- Put generated metrics, reports, and artifacts in `results/`.
